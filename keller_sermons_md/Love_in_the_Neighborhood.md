## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Sermon on the Mount

This month on the podcast, we are featuring a series on the sermon on the Mount, a collection of teachings where Jesus outlines what it really means to live the Christian life. In the series, Tim Keller explores a striking idea that Christianity is not just different from the world, it's also radically different from religion.

> "But I tell you who hear me, love your enemies, do good to those who hate you, bless those who curse you, pray for those who mistreat you."

**Christianity is inside out.**

### Two Principles for Understanding

First of all, the sermon on the mount must not be read individually or moralistically. Now by individualistically we mean this. The sermon on the mount talks about how to conduct our relationships, how to treat our attitude toward our money, and so on.

- The sermon on the mount is about a kingdom.
- It's talking about an alternate community.

### Three Groups of People

#### 1. People Who Oppose You

Love your enemies, those who hate you, those who curse you.

#### 2. People Less Fortunate Than You

> "Give to everyone who asks you."

#### 3. People Who Are Indifferent

Even sinners love those who love them.

### The Difficulty and Dynamic of Love

Three times, he says, if you love only those who love you, what credit is that?

- **Your love isn't love.**
- You are an investor sucking air when it comes to love.

### Practical Directions

Turn the other cheek, and then forgive.

- **Oppose injustice, but do it without any inner touchiness about your ego.**
- Look around your neighborhood. Do you ignore them and just go after the Christians?

> "Until you are amazed with the mercy he gave, you will never be empowered to provide the mercy he requires."

Let's pray. We thank you, Lord, for this kind of resource. This is dynamite. This is like a fuse. All we have to do is light it. But now help us to light it.

## 中文翻译

### 引言：登山宝训

本月的播客中，我们推出了一系列关于登山宝训的讲道，这是耶稣阐述真正基督徒生活意义的教导合集。在这个系列中，Tim Keller 探讨了一个引人注目的观点：基督教不仅与世界不同，而且与宗教截然不同。

> “但我告诉你们这些听见的人，要爱你们的仇敌，善待那些恨你们的人，祝福那些咒诅你们的人，为那些虐待你们的人祷告。”

**基督教是由内而外的。**

### 理解的两个原则

首先，登山宝训不能被个别地或道德主义地阅读。所谓个别地是指，登山宝训谈论如何处理我们的关系，如何对待我们的金钱态度，等等。

- 登山宝训是关于一个国度的。
- 它谈论的是一个替代性的社区。

### 三类人群

#### 1. 反对你的人

爱你的仇敌，那些恨你的人，那些咒诅你的人。

#### 2. 比你不幸的人

> “给每一个向你要的人。”

#### 3. 冷漠的人

即使是罪人也爱那些爱他们的人。

### 爱的困难与动力

他三次说，如果你只爱那些爱你的人，那有什么功劳呢？

- **你的爱不是爱。**
- 在爱的问题上，你是一个吸气的投资者。

### 实际指引

转过另一边脸，然后原谅。

- **反对不公正，但要做到内心没有任何关于自我的触动。**
- 环顾你的邻里。你是否忽视他们，只追求基督徒？

> “直到你对他所给予的怜悯感到惊讶，你才会有能力提供他所要求的怜悯。”

让我们祷告。我们感谢你，主，为这种资源。这是炸药。这就像一个引线。我们所要做的就是点燃它。但现在请帮助我们点燃它。
