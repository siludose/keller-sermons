## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Sermon on the Mount

This month on the podcast, we are featuring a series on the Sermon on the Mount, a collection of teachings where Jesus outlines what it really means to live the Christian life. In the series, Tim Keller explores a striking idea that Christianity is not just different from the world, it's also radically different from religion.

> "Take a look in your bulletin, we have a passage on which the teachings based, we're going through the Sermon on the Mount, and we come to what Jesus has to say about money and possessions, and he has plenty to say, more than we can cover, but let's read it."

**Matthew 6: Do not store up for yourselves treasures on earth, where moth and rust destroy, where thieves break in and steal, but store up for yourselves treasures in heaven, where moth and rust do not destroy, and where thieves do not break in and steal, for where your treasure is, there your heart will be also.**

### How Money Exercises Power Over Us

Jesus tells us three things that we can draw out here. He tells us how money controls us, you know, how it has a power over us, how it exercises power.

#### 1. The Eye as the Lamp of the Body

The eye is the lamp of the body. If your eyes are good, your whole body will be full of light, but if your eyes are bad, your whole body will be full of darkness. If then the light within you is darkness, how great is that darkness?

> "No one can serve two masters. He will hate the one and love the other, or he will be devoted to the one and despise the other. You cannot serve both God and money."

### Why Money Exercises Power Over Us

#### 1. Money as Significance

Money serves the way in which you actually, the place where your heart really rests, is revealed by money. So what it means is, money is a way of getting significance for many of us.

#### 2. Money as Security

Some people use money for approval, other people use money for control. Some people use money in order to say I feel important. But many other people say I need money, so I'm safe.

### How to Break the Power of Money

#### 1. Treasuring Jesus

The Bible says, every treasure but Jesus will insist that you die to purchase it. But Jesus himself is the one treasure who died to purchase you.

> "How do you make Jesus Christ your treasure? Only if you make him your treasure. Only if he is your heavenly treasure. Only if you treasure him."

#### 2. Generosity as a Sign

If Jesus is your treasure, you'll love the rich. If Jesus is your treasure, you'll love the poor. If Jesus is the treasure, you'll be giving your money away joyfully, deliberately, happily.

### Conclusion

Our Father, I thank You for giving us this time to consider what it means to treasure up treasures in heaven. We thank You for what Your Son did for us. We thank You for all of that. And we pray that You would help it become such a reality in our lives that money no longer has power over us.

> "The intimidation we feel around people who have more money, or the inferiority we feel, or even the anger we feel, or the superiority we feel. Oh, Lord, change that so that we are very different kinds of people."

**We are unique people. You've done it in the past. The early church really understood this. Let it be true of us, too. We pray in Jesus' name. Amen.**

## 中文翻译

### 引言：登山宝训

本月的播客中，我们推出了一系列关于登山宝训的讲道，这是耶稣阐述真正基督徒生活意义的一系列教导。在这个系列中，Tim Keller 探讨了一个引人注目的观点，即基督教不仅与世界不同，而且与宗教也有根本的不同。

> "看看你的公告，我们有一段经文是这些教导的基础，我们正在研读登山宝训，现在我们来到耶稣关于金钱和财物的教导，他有很多话要说，超过我们能涵盖的，但让我们来读一下。"

**马太福音 6: 不要为自己积攒财宝在地上，地上有虫子咬，能锈坏，也有贼挖窟窿来偷。只要积攒财宝在天上，天上没有虫子咬，不能锈坏，也没有贼挖窟窿来偷。因为你的财宝在哪里，你的心也在哪里。**

### 金钱如何对我们施加影响

耶稣在这里告诉我们三件事。他告诉我们金钱如何控制我们，如何对我们施加影响。

#### 1. 眼睛是身体的灯

眼睛是身体的灯。你的眼睛若了亮，全身就光明；你的眼睛若昏花，全身就黑暗。你里头的光若黑暗了，那黑暗是何等大呢？

> "没有人能侍奉两个主。他不是恶这个爱那个，就是重这个轻那个。你们不能又侍奉神，又侍奉玛门。"

### 金钱为何对我们有影响

#### 1. 金钱作为意义

金钱是你心灵真正安息之处的体现。对许多人来说，金钱是一种获得意义的方式。

#### 2. 金钱作为安全感

有些人用金钱来获得认可，另一些人用金钱来获得控制。有些人用金钱来证明自己很重要，但许多人说我需要钱，所以我才安全。

### 如何打破金钱的影响力

#### 1. 珍视耶稣

圣经说，除了耶稣之外的每一个珍宝都会要求你为之付出生命。但耶稣自己是那个为你付出生命的珍宝。

> "你如何让耶稣基督成为你的珍宝？只有当你让他成为你的珍宝时，只有当他是你的天上珍宝时，只有当你珍视他时。"

#### 2. 慷慨作为标志

如果耶稣是你的珍宝，你会爱富人。如果耶稣是你的珍宝，你会爱穷人。如果耶稣是珍宝，你会愉快地、有计划地、快乐地把钱捐出去。

### 结论

我们的父神，我感谢你赐给我们这个时间来思考什么是积攒天上的财宝。我们感谢你儿子为我们所做的一切。我们祈求你帮助这些成为我们生活中的现实，使金钱不再对我们有影响力。

> "我们在有钱人面前感到的恐惧，或我们感到的自卑，甚至是我们感到的愤怒，或我们感到的优越感。哦，主啊，改变这一切，使我们成为非常不同的人。"

**我们是独特的人。你过去已经做到这一点。早期教会真正理解了这一点。愿这也成为我们的真实。我们奉耶稣的名祷告。阿门。**
