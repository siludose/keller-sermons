## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Challenge of Public Faith

How do we share what it means to truly know Jesus? Not just as a historical figure or moral teacher, but as Savior and King. This month, Tim Keller explores what the Bible shows us about being public with our faith and sharing the hope we have in Christ.

Tonight's Scripture comes from a book of John. Chapter 4, verses 27 through 42.

> "Just then his disciples returned and were surprised to find him talking with a woman. But no one asked, what do you want or why are you talking with her?"

**We live in a very pluralistic society, New York particularly. Your neighbor probably holds deeply sharply divergent beliefs about things to your beliefs.**

### The Call to Spiritual Sowing

Jesus talks to his disciples and he gets real metaphorical and he talks about spiritual sewing, seed and reaping. Now what is that? What is he trying to get across in that metaphor?

- **Spiritual sowing is pointing people to eternal life through Jesus Christ.**
- Spiritual reaping is seeing some of them believe.

### The Method of Public Faith

Look at what she's doing. It says, leaving her water jar, the woman went back to the town and said to the people, "Come see a man who told me everything I ever did. Could this be the Messiah?"

- **Simple transparency and pointing to Jesus himself.**
- She doesn't get up on a box and start preaching.

### The Power and Motivation

Jesus, of course, is pointing this woman away from her natural beliefs to new beliefs. But look how He's doing it.

- **The real question is whose truth claims are going to lead most to peace?**
- Jesus engages her with love, not pride.

> "He saw me to the bottom, but loved me to the skies."

**Let's pray.**

So Father, thank you so much for eternal life. It comes to us through the thirst, the cosmic thirst, the death of Jesus Christ. We do want to be part of the solution. We live in a pluralistic society. We desperately need people who know how to share who they are, that is disarming, respectful, loving, brave. And we pray, Father, you would make us that, those kinds of people. Thank you for giving this to us through the grace of Jesus Christ who came not to be served, but to serve, and to give his life a ransom for many. His name we pray. Amen.

## 中文翻译

### 引言：公开信仰的挑战

我们如何分享真正认识耶稣的意义？不仅仅是作为一个历史人物或道德教师，而是作为救主和君王。这个月，Tim Keller 探讨了圣经如何指导我们公开我们的信仰，并分享我们在基督里的希望。

今晚的经文来自约翰福音。第四章，27到42节。

> “就在那时，他的门徒回来了，惊讶地发现他正在和一个女人说话。但没有人问，你想要什么，或者你为什么和她说话？”

**我们生活在一个非常多元化的社会，尤其是纽约。你的邻居可能持有与你的信仰截然不同的信念。**

### 属灵播种的呼召

耶稣与他的门徒交谈，他变得非常比喻化，他谈论属灵的播种、种子和收割。那是什么？他在这个比喻中试图传达什么？

- **属灵的播种是指向人们通过耶稣基督获得永生。**
- 属灵的收割是看到其中一些人相信。

### 公开信仰的方法

看看她在做什么。经上说，她留下水罐，回到城里，对人们说：“来看看一个告诉我一切我所做过的事的人。难道这就是弥赛亚吗？”

- **简单的透明性和指向耶稣本身。**
- 她没有站在箱子上开始讲道。

### 力量与动机

当然，耶稣正在将这个女人从她的自然信仰引导到新的信仰。但看看他是如何做到的。

- **真正的问题是谁的真理主张将最有可能带来和平？**
- 耶稣以爱而非骄傲与她交流。

> “他看到了我的底线，但爱我至高。”

**让我们祷告。**

父啊，感谢你赐给我们永生。它通过耶稣基督的渴望，宇宙的渴望，死亡而来到我们这里。我们确实想成为解决方案的一部分。我们生活在一个多元化的社会中。我们迫切需要知道如何分享自己的人，这种人是解除武装的，尊重的，充满爱的，勇敢的。我们祈祷，父啊，你会让我们成为这样的人。感谢你通过耶稣基督的恩典赐给我们这一切，他来不是要受人服侍，而是要服侍人，并为多人舍命作赎价。我们奉他的名祷告。阿门。
