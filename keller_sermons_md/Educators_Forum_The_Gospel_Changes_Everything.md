## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction

Tim Keller discusses the impact of the Gospel on education, emphasizing how it reshapes our approach to teaching and learning. He reflects on his own experience as a teacher and the challenges faced by educators in integrating faith with public education.

### Part 1: The Essence of Modern Education

Keller references C.S. Lewis's "The Abolition of Man," highlighting the modern tendency to separate objective facts from subjective values. He argues that this division creates "men without chests," individuals lacking moral and spiritual depth.

> "We make men without chests and expect of them virtue and enterprise."

### Part 2: The Role of Common Grace

Keller explains the concept of common grace, emphasizing that all humans have an innate understanding of right and wrong. He discusses how this understanding can be used to educate in both Christian and public schools.

### Part 3: Education Choices

Keller shares his personal experience with different types of schooling for his children, advocating for a case-by-case approach. He stresses the importance of Christians being involved in all forms of education to bring renewal.

### Part 4: The Importance of Community

He emphasizes that character is formed through community, not just individual instruction. Schools should foster environments where students see virtues modeled and reinforced.

### Conclusion

Keller encourages educators to persevere in their calling, reminding them that their work is part of God's redemptive plan. He assures them that despite the challenges, their efforts contribute to the reweaving of creation.

## 中文翻译

### 引言

提姆·凯勒讨论了福音对教育的影响，强调它如何重塑我们对教学和学习的态度。他反思了自己作为教师的经历，以及教育者在将信仰融入公共教育中所面临的挑战。

### 第一部分：现代教育的本质

凯勒引用了C.S.路易斯的《人的废除》，强调现代倾向于将客观事实与主观价值分开。他认为这种分裂创造了“没有胸膛的人”，即缺乏道德和精神深度的个体。

> “我们制造没有胸膛的人，却期望他们有美德和进取心。”

### 第二部分：普遍恩典的角色

凯勒解释了普遍恩典的概念，强调所有人类都有对错的内在理解。他讨论了这种理解如何可以用于基督教和公立学校的教育。

### 第三部分：教育选择

凯勒分享了他在为孩子选择不同类型学校时的个人经验，倡导根据具体情况做出选择。他强调基督徒参与所有形式教育以带来更新的重要性。

### 第四部分：社区的重要性

他强调品格是通过社区而非单独的教学形成的。学校应营造学生能看到美德被示范和强化的环境。

### 结语

凯勒鼓励教育者坚持他们的使命，提醒他们他们的工作是上帝救赎计划的一部分。他向他们保证，尽管有挑战，他们的努力有助于重塑创造。
