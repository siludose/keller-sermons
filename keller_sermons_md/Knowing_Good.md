## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Deepest Questions

When someone you know is contemplating life's deepest questions, who am I? What's wrong with the world? What can truly make me whole? Jesus doesn't just give us answers. He gives us Himself. In this month's podcast, Tim Keller looks at how we can share the hope we have in Christ as the answer to a person's search for meaning and purpose.

> "Jesus doesn't just give us answers. He gives us Himself."

**Jesus is the answer to life's deepest questions.**

### The Problem of Relativism

#### 1. No One Can Succeed in Being a Relativist

Now, verse 12, all who sin apart from the law will also perish apart from the law, and all who sin under the law will be judged by the law. Now who is he talking about? He's talking about the law. He means by the law, the moral law of God that's revealed in the Bible in the scriptures.

- God, the judge, will not judge you according to standards you don't accept.
- Even if you don't have the Bible as your moral standard, you still know something about the moral law.
- No one is really a relativist.

> "No one can succeed in being a relativist."

**Everyone has some understanding of the moral law of God.**

### The Problem of Moralism

#### 2. No One Can Succeed in Being a Moralist

Now you who call yourself a Jew, if you rely on the law, now he turns to people who say, okay, I do believe the Bible, I absolutely believe the Bible, and I accept the Bible, and I'm living according to the Bible.

- Hypocrisy: No one lives up to their own standards.
- Idolatry: Living for things other than God.

> "You then who teach others, do you not teach yourself?"

**Hypocrisy and idolatry prevent moralists from succeeding.**

### The Only Hope: A New Heart

A person is not a Jew who is one outwardly, nor is circumcision merely outward and physical. No, a person is a Jew who is one inwardly and circumcision is circumcision of the heart by the spirit, not by the written code.

- The only hope is a new heart.
- Circumcision of the heart means the new birth.

> "The only hope is a new heart."

**In Jesus Christ, the judge who was judged, we have hope.**

## 中文翻译

### 引言：最深刻的问题

当你认识的人在思考人生最深刻的问题时，我是谁？世界出了什么问题？什么能真正使我完整？耶稣不仅给我们答案，他把自己给了我们。在本月的播客中，Tim Keller 探讨了我们如何分享我们在基督里的希望，作为一个人寻找意义和目的的答案。

> "耶稣不仅给我们答案，他把自己给了我们。"

**耶稣是人生最深刻问题的答案。**

### 相对主义的问题

#### 1. 没有人能成功成为相对主义者

现在，第12节，所有在律法之外犯罪的人也将在律法之外灭亡，所有在律法之下犯罪的人将被律法审判。他在谈论谁？他在谈论律法。他所指的律法是上帝在圣经中启示的道德律法。

- 上帝，审判者，不会根据你不接受的标准来审判你。
- 即使你没有圣经作为你的道德标准，你仍然知道一些关于道德律法的事情。
- 没有人是真正的相对主义者。

> "没有人能成功成为相对主义者。"

**每个人对上帝的道德律法都有一些理解。**

### 道德主义的问题

#### 2. 没有人能成功成为道德主义者

现在你自称是犹太人，如果你依靠律法，现在他转向那些说，好吧，我相信圣经，我绝对相信圣经，我接受圣经，并且我按照圣经生活的人。

- 伪善：没有人能达到自己的标准。
- 偶像崇拜：为上帝以外的事物而活。

> "你教导别人，难道不教导自己吗？"

**伪善和偶像崇拜阻止道德主义者成功。**

### 唯一的希望：新的心

一个人不是外在的犹太人，割礼也不仅仅是外在和身体上的。不，一个人是内在的犹太人，割礼是心灵的割礼，由圣灵而不是书写的律法。

- 唯一的希望是新的心。
- 心灵的割礼意味着重生。

> "唯一的希望是新的心。"

**在耶稣基督里，被审判的审判者，我们有希望。**
