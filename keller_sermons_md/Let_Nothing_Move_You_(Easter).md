## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Importance of Believing in the Resurrection

Welcome to the Gospel and Life podcast. Do you believe Christ's resurrection is merely a symbol or an actual real historical event? Today, Tim Keller is teaching on why believing in the historical reality of the resurrection is the only way it will really change you.

> "After you listen to today's teaching, we invite you to go online to gospelandlife.com and sign up for email updates."

**The resurrection must be believed as a historical event to truly impact your life.**

### The Historical Reality of the Resurrection

Now, there's three basic things that Paul says about the resurrection. First Corinthians 15. For what I received, I passed on to you as of first importance, that Christ died for our sins according to the Scriptures, that He was buried, that He was raised on the third day according to the Scriptures, and that He appeared to Peter, and then to the twelve.

> "But Christ has indeed been raised from the dead."

Paul emphasizes the importance of believing in the actual resurrection.

### What the Resurrection Accomplished

The resurrection isn't just something that happened. It's not just a naked display of power, it accomplishes something. Paul says, if Christ has not raised from the dead, you're still in your sins.

> "Jesus was put to death for our sins, but he was raised for our justification."

**The resurrection means condemnation is over.**

### How the Resurrection Should Change Us

Therefore, my dear brothers, stand firm, let nothing move you. Always give yourselves fully to the work of the Lord.

> "How do you deal with trouble? How do you become strong?"

**The resurrection gives us hope and strength to face life's challenges.**

### Conclusion

Let nothing move you. Therefore, do you understand this? To the degree you do, nothing will move you.

Let's pray. Father, we ask that you would make us these strong, immutable people, because of the hope that has pierced our heart like a shaft. Amen.

## 中文翻译

### 引言：相信复活的重要性

欢迎来到福音与生命播客。你相信基督的复活仅仅是一个象征，还是一个真实的历史事件？今天，Tim Keller 将讲述为什么相信复活的历史真实性是它真正改变你的唯一途径。

> “在你听完今天的讲道后，我们邀请你在线访问 gospelandlife.com 并注册电子邮件更新。”

**复活必须被相信为一个历史事件，才能真正影响你的生活。**

### 复活的历史真实性

现在，保罗关于复活有三件基本的事情要说。哥林多前书 15。因为我所领受的，已经传给你们，首先就是基督照圣经所说，为我们的罪死了，埋葬了，第三天复活了，并且显现给彼得，然后给十二使徒。

> “但基督确实从死里复活了。”

保罗强调相信实际复活的重要性。

### 复活成就了什么

复活不仅仅是发生的事情。它不仅仅是权力的裸露展示，它成就了一些事情。保罗说，如果基督没有从死里复活，你们仍在罪中。

> “耶稣为我们的罪被处死，但他为我们的称义而复活。”

**复活意味着定罪已经结束。**

### 复活如何改变我们

因此，我亲爱的弟兄们，要坚定不移，让任何事都不能动摇你们。总是全心全意地为主的工作。

> “你如何处理麻烦？你如何变得坚强？”

**复活给了我们面对生活挑战的希望和力量。**

### 结论

让任何事都不能动摇你。因此，你明白这一点了吗？在你理解的程度上，任何事都不能动摇你。

让我们祷告。父啊，我们祈求你使我们成为这些坚强、不动摇的人，因为希望像箭一样刺穿了我们的心。阿门。
