## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Sermon on the Mount

This month on the podcast, we are featuring a series on the Sermon on the Mount, a collection of teachings where Jesus outlines what it really means to live the Christian life. In the series, Tim Keller explores a striking idea that Christianity is not just different from the world, it's also radically different from religion.

> "He went down with them and stood on a level place. A large crowd of his disciples were there and a great number of people from all over Judea from Jerusalem and from the coast of Tyre and Sidon who had come to hear him and be healed of their diseases."

**Blessed are you who are poor for yours is the kingdom of God. Blessed are you who hunger now for you will be satisfied. Blessed are you who weep now for you will laugh. Blessed are you when men hate you and when they exclude you and insult you and reject your name as evil because of the Son of Man. Rejoice in that day and leap for joy because great is your reward in heaven.**

### The Right Side Up Kingdom

#### 1. The Values of the Right Side Up Kingdom

In the kingdom Christ is replacing, which is outlined in verses 24 to 26, the values are power, comfort, success, and recognition.

- Power: Riches and poverty are really a matter of power.
- Comfort: Material comfort, nice clothes, great restaurants, beautiful home.
- Success: The word for laugh here means to gloat, to celebrate victory over others.
- Recognition: Acclaim, popularity, celebrity.

#### 2. The Power of the Right Side Up Kingdom

The power of this kingdom is the power of the now. Wealth, power, success, and comfort are things of the now.

> "If the now is all there really is, if this world is all there is, absolutely. This set of values seems absolutely natural."

### The Upside Down Kingdom

#### 1. The Reversal of Values

In the life of God's people, there will be a remarkable reversal of values. The people of God will prize what the world calls pitiable and suspect what the world thinks desirable.

- Weakness, sacrifice, grief, and exclusion are prized.
- Christians are no longer controlled by power, success, comfort, or recognition.

#### 2. The Power of the Upside Down Kingdom

The power comes from knowing that Christ reversed places with us. He took our place, and we stand in his place.

> "Blessed are you who are poor."

**The only way to receive this power is to be poor in spirit.**

### Pattern, Power, and Product of the Upside Down Kingdom

#### 1. The Pattern

The pattern of the upside down kingdom is a reversal of values with regard to the world.

- Christians are not controlled by the world's values.
- They can take or leave power, success, comfort, and recognition.

#### 2. The Power

The power comes from Christ's example and his substitution for us.

- Jesus became poor and was rejected, even by his Father.
- He reversed fortunes with us, giving us power.

#### 3. The Product

The product is a life lived with integrity and courage.

- Christians live recklessly by the world's standards, not afraid of weeping or emptiness.
- They know the kingdom of God makes progress in their lives and others' lives when these things happen.

### Conclusion

The handwriting is on the wall for the old kingdom. We must ask ourselves if we are living a life controlled by the world's values or by Christ's example.

> "Don't be like Belshazzar, be like Nebuchadnezzar. Live in the upside down kingdom, not in the right side up kingdom."

**Let's pray for the ability to apply this in our lives, to be humbled and live according to the upside down kingdom.**

## 中文翻译

### 引言：登山宝训

本月的播客中，我们推出了一系列关于登山宝训的讲道，这是耶稣教导的一系列内容，阐明了真正的基督徒生活意味着什么。在这个系列中，Tim Keller 探讨了一个引人注目的观点，即基督教不仅与世界不同，而且与宗教也有根本的不同。

> "他和他们一起下山，站在一块平地上。他的门徒有很多，还有来自犹太全地、耶路撒冷以及推罗和西顿海岸的许多人来听他讲道并治愈他们的疾病。"

**你们贫穷的人有福了，因为神的国是你们的。你们现在饥饿的人有福了，因为你们将得饱足。你们现在哭泣的人有福了，因为你们将欢笑。人因人子恨你们、排斥你们、辱骂你们、弃绝你们的名为恶，你们就有福了。在那日要欢喜跳跃，因为你们在天上的赏赐是大的。**

### 正立的国度

#### 1. 正立国度的价值观

在基督要取代的国度中，正如在24到26节中所描述的，价值观是权力、舒适、成功和认可。

- 权力：财富和贫穷实际上是权力的问题。
- 舒适：物质舒适，漂亮的衣服，豪华的餐厅，美丽的家。
- 成功：这里的“笑”一词意味着幸灾乐祸，庆祝胜利。
- 认可：赞誉，名声，名望。

#### 2. 正立国度的力量

这个国度的力量是现在的力量。财富、权力、成功和舒适是现在的事物。

> "如果现在是唯一真实的，如果这个世界就是一切，那么这些价值观似乎绝对自然。"

### 颠倒的国度

#### 1. 价值观的逆转

在神的子民的生活中，将会出现一个显著的价值观逆转。神的子民将珍视世界认为可怜的东西，并怀疑世界认为可取的东西。

- 弱点、牺牲、悲伤和排斥被珍视。
- 基督徒不再被权力、成功、舒适或认可所控制。

#### 2. 颠倒国度的力量

力量来自于知道基督与我们交换了位置。他取代了我们的位置，我们站在他的位置上。

> "贫穷的人有福了。"

**获得这种力量的唯一途径是灵里贫穷。**

### 颠倒国度的模式、力量和结果

#### 1. 模式

颠倒国度的模式是对世界价值观的逆转。

- 基督徒不被世界的价值观所控制。
- 他们可以接受或放弃权力、成功、舒适和认可。

#### 2. 力量

力量来自基督的榜样和他为我们的替代。

- 耶稣变得贫穷并被拒绝，甚至被他的父亲拒绝。
- 他与我们交换了命运，给予我们力量。

#### 3. 结果

结果是过着正直和勇敢的生活。

- 基督徒以世界的标准过着无所畏惧的生活，不怕哭泣或空虚。
- 他们知道当这些事情发生时，神的国在他们的生活和他人的生活中取得进展。

### 结论

旧国度的末日已到。我们必须问自己，我们是过着被世界价值观控制的生活，还是以基督的榜样生活。

> "不要像伯沙撒，要像尼布甲尼撒。生活在颠倒的国度中，而不是正立的国度中。"

**让我们祈祷有能力在我们的生活中应用这一点，谦卑地生活在颠倒的国度中。**
