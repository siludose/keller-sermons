## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Role of the Holy Spirit

Welcome to Gospel and Life! Who is the Holy Spirit? And why is the Spirit's work so vital to the Christian life? The Bible tells us the Holy Spirit is not just a vague force, but a person who works in the lives of Christians in profound ways. Today Tim Keller is exploring how the Spirit calls us to faith, unites us together, equips us with gifts and shapes us to be more like Christ.

Tonight's scripture is from the Book of Romans, chapter 8, verses 15 through 27.

> For you did not receive a Spirit that makes you a slave again to fear, but you received the Spirit of sonship, and by him we cry, Abba, Father. The Spirit Himself testifies with our spirits that we are God's children.

**The Spirit calls us and enables us to believe.**

### The Future Work of the Spirit

#### 1. Glorification

Romans 8 is an amazing chapter, and a little further down on chapter 8 and verse 30, we read where it says, "He, the Lord, those he called, he also justified, but those he justified, he also glorified." He doesn't just stop at justification, he justified as he also glorifies. We are going to be glorified.

- **We have been saved, but we will be saved.**
- Redemption means to be purchased, ransomed out of bondage.

#### 2. Redemption of Our Bodies

The Greeks believed the body was bad, but the Bible sees it differently. **Your final redemption is not even going to heaven.** Your final redemption is at the end of time when you're resurrected and you have a new and glorious body.

### The Present Help of the Spirit

#### 1. Assurance

The Spirit bears witness with our spirits that we're children of God. It shores us that we're His children. **The Spirit gives us such confidence that God loves us.**

#### 2. Intercession

Sometimes we don't even know what to pray for. **The Spirit Himself intercedes for us with groans that words cannot express.**

### The Assurance of Hope

**Hope is an assured future.** What you know about the future completely determines how you process the present.

- **The Spirit groans with us.**
- Jesus groaned in pain and God abandoned him so that you can know, whenever you groan, God will not abandon you.

Let us pray.

Our Father, help us take the theology of this passage. It should make us zealous for caring for material needs. It should make us powerful in the face of our own sickness and even impending death. It should fill us with such hope that we are able to face the worst groaning and suffering because we know that someday your spirit will end it all. Father, we like the rest of creation long for the final redemption of our bodies and until then, holy spirit, help us, help us to have the joy and the peace of past understanding and the midst of what we have to face. Thank you for all these assurances. Make us people with zeal and joy but who also know how to weep with those who weep because we know these great truths, we pray this in Jesus' name, Amen.

## 中文翻译

### 引言：圣灵的角色

欢迎来到福音与生活！圣灵是谁？为什么圣灵的工作对基督徒的生活如此重要？圣经告诉我们，圣灵不仅仅是一个模糊的力量，而是一个在基督徒生活中以深刻方式工作的位格。今天，Tim Keller 探讨圣灵如何呼召我们信仰，将我们联合在一起，装备我们以恩赐，并塑造我们更像基督。

今晚的经文来自罗马书第八章，第15到27节。

> 因为你们所受的，不是奴仆的灵，仍旧害怕；所受的乃是儿子的灵，因此我们呼叫：阿爸，父！圣灵与我们的心同证我们是神的儿女。

**圣灵呼召我们并使我们能够相信。**

### 圣灵未来的工作

#### 1. 荣耀化

罗马书第八章是一个惊人的章节，在第八章和第30节，我们读到：“他所召的那些人，又称他们为义；所称为义的人，又叫他们得荣耀。”他不仅仅停在称义，他称义的同时也使我们得荣耀。我们将要得荣耀。

- **我们已经得救，但我们将要得救。**
- 救赎意味着被购买，从束缚中赎出。

#### 2. 我们身体的救赎

希腊人认为身体是坏的，但圣经有不同的看法。**你最终的救赎甚至不是去天堂。**你最终的救赎是在时间的尽头，当你复活并拥有一个新的荣耀的身体时。

### 圣灵现在的帮助

#### 1. 确信

圣灵与我们的心同证我们是神的儿女。它确保我们是他的孩子。**圣灵给我们如此的信心，知道神爱我们。**

#### 2. 代求

有时我们甚至不知道该祷告什么。**圣灵亲自用说不出来的叹息替我们祷告。**

### 盼望的确据

**盼望是一个确定的未来。**你对未来的了解完全决定了你如何处理现在。

- **圣灵与我们一同叹息。**
- 耶稣在痛苦中叹息，神离弃了他，以便你可以知道，无论何时你叹息，神都不会离弃你。

让我们祷告。

我们的父，帮助我们理解这段经文的神学。它应该使我们热心关心物质需求。它应该使我们在面对自己的疾病甚至即将到来的死亡时充满力量。它应该充满我们如此的盼望，使我们能够面对最严重的叹息和痛苦，因为我们知道有一天你的灵会结束这一切。父啊，我们像其他受造物一样，渴望我们身体的最终救赎，直到那时，圣灵，帮助我们，帮助我们在我们必须面对的事情中拥有超越理解的喜乐与平安。感谢你给予我们的所有保证。使我们成为充满热情和喜乐的人，但也知道如何与那些哭泣的人同哭，因为我们知道这些伟大的真理，我们奉耶稣的名祷告，阿门。
