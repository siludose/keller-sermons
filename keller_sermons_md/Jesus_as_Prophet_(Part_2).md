## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: Christianity's Essence

Christianity isn't just a spiritual practice or set of moral teachings. At its heart, it's the person of Jesus actively pursuing us. In today's teaching, Tim Keller unpacks how Jesus actively seeks us, reveals truth to us, and calls us to Himself.

> "Everything must be fulfilled that is written about me in the law of Moses, the prophets, and the Psalms."

**The Bible describes Christianity as something that comes upon you, not something you choose.** It's a force that argues with you, seeks you out, and makes you think about things you don't want to think about.

### Jesus as the Revealer

Jesus is not a passive Savior. He is not just a priest, but a prophet and a revealer. After the resurrection, He comes to His disciples and opens their minds. **He continues to do this today through the Holy Spirit.**

> "You have not chosen me, I have chosen you."

### The Prophetic Ministry of Christ

#### 1. What He Does

He gives us eyes. Historically, the church has understood Jesus' prophetic ministry as having two parts: earthly and heavenly. **He provides both the external word and the internal word.**

- External word: His teachings on earth.
- Internal word: The Holy Spirit opening our minds.

#### 2. Why He Does It

Because we are spiritually blind. The human mind is like a mountain, impenetrable until Jesus breaks through.

> "He opened their minds so they could understand the scriptures."

### Why We Need Jesus' Revelation

Spiritual blindness is not a lack of reason but an inability to value and appreciate the truth. **It's about seeing the value of what Jesus has done for us.**

### How Jesus Opens Our Eyes

He makes connections. **Understanding means seeing the connections between the pieces of truth.**

- Newness: There's always a newness about what you're learning.
- Engagement: Involves mind, will, and emotions.
- Gradual: It's a gradual unfolding of understanding.

### Application and Conclusion

Some of you may not realize the holistic nature of Christianity. **You need Jesus to teach your heart.**

- Pray for illumination.
- Seek understanding and engage with the truth.

> "The moment you say 'I don't see,' that moment you begin to see."

Let's pray for humility and the desire for spiritual work.

> "Father, we ask that you would help us, first of all, to see that we don't."

Amen.

## 中文翻译

### 引言：基督教的本质

基督教不仅仅是一种灵修实践或道德教导。其核心是耶稣这个人积极地追求我们。在今天的教导中，Tim Keller 解释了耶稣如何积极地寻找我们，向我们揭示真理，并呼召我们归向祂。

> “凡指着我在摩西的律法、先知书和诗篇上所记的话都必须应验。”

**圣经描述基督教为一种降临于你的东西，而不是你选择的东西。** 这是一种与你争辩、寻找你的力量，使你思考你不愿意思考的事情。

### 耶稣作为启示者

耶稣不是一个被动的救主。祂不仅是祭司，还是先知和启示者。复活后，祂来到门徒面前，开启他们的心灵。**祂今天通过圣灵继续这样做。**

> “不是你们拣选了我，是我拣选了你们。”

### 基督的先知职事

#### 1. 祂做了什么

祂给我们眼睛。历史上，教会理解耶稣的先知职事有两个部分：地上的和天上的。**祂提供外在的话语和内在的话语。**

- 外在的话语：祂在地上的教导。
- 内在的话语：圣灵开启我们的心灵。

#### 2. 为什么祂这样做

因为我们在灵里是盲目的。人类的心灵就像一座山，直到耶稣突破。

> “祂开启他们的心灵，使他们能明白圣经。”

### 为什么我们需要耶稣的启示

灵里的盲目不是缺乏理性，而是无法重视和欣赏真理。**这关乎看到耶稣为我们所做之事的价值。**

### 耶稣如何开启我们的眼睛

祂建立联系。**理解意味着看到真理之间的联系。**

- 新鲜感：你所学的总是有新鲜感。
- 参与：涉及心智、意志和情感。
- 渐进：这是一个逐渐展开的理解。

### 应用与结论

有些人可能没有意识到基督教的整体性。**你需要耶稣教导你的心。**

- 祈求启示。
- 寻求理解并与真理互动。

> “当你说‘我看不见’的那一刻，你就开始看见了。”

让我们祈求谦卑和对属灵工作的渴望。

> “父啊，我们请求你首先帮助我们看到我们看不见。”

阿们。
