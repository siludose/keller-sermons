# Love Your Enemies

## Sermon Outline / 讲道大纲

1. Introduction: Invitation to Gospel in Life partnership and introduction to sermon theme.
   介绍：邀请参与福音生活伙伴关系并介绍布道主题。

2. Understanding the Ten Commandments as a framework for greatness.
   将十诫理解为伟大的框架。

3. Jesus' exposition on the Sixth Commandment: Moving beyond physical murder to the heart attitude.
   耶稣对第六诫的讲解：超越肉体谋杀，关注内心态度。

4. The radical ethic of love: Turn the other cheek.
   爱的激进伦理：转过脸颊。

5. Christianity as an interconnected set of radically altered relationships.
   基督教作为一系列紧密相连的、彻底改变的关系。

6. The progression: Relationship with God, self, and others.
   进展：与上帝、自我及他人的关系。

7. Jesus' teachings on anger and contempt as the root of murder.
   耶稣关于愤怒和蔑视作为谋杀根源的教导。

8. Practical application: Restructuring personal attitudes to embody love.
   实际应用：重构个人态度以体现爱。
