# Overflowing Joy

## Sermon Outline / 讲道大纲

1. Introduction: The Ten Commandments as high steel for building a great life
   介绍：十诫是建造伟大人生的钢架

2. The Eighth Commandment: Thou shalt not steal - Money and power
   第八诫：不可偷盗——金钱与权力

3. Money was designed to be our dignity, but sin made it our definition
   金钱本是为了我们的尊严，但罪使它成为我们的定义

4. Three principles for financial liberation:
   财务自由的三个原则：
   - God owns everything - you are a trustee (神拥有一切——你是管家)
   - Jesus impoverished himself for you - radical generosity (耶稣为你成为贫穷——激进的慷慨)
   - You can take it with you - invest in eternal things (你可以带走它——投资永恒的事物)

5. Two kinds of stealing:
   两种偷盗：
   - Not honoring others' caretaker rights (不尊重他人的管理权)
   - Not honoring God's rights over your wealth (不尊重神对你财富的权利)

6. Guidelines for giving: tithe, lifestyle change, excitement
   奉献的准则：十一奉献、改变生活方式、热情

7. Conclusion: Robert Murray M'Cheyne on Christ-like giving
   结论：慕安德烈论效法基督的给予
