# First of All

## Sermon Outline / 讲道大纲

1. Introduction to Gospel in Life and Monthly Partnership
   介绍福音生活和月度合作伙伴

2. Understanding the Purpose of the Ten Commandments
   理解十诫的目的

3. Jonah's Anger and God's Compassion
   约拿的愤怒与神的怜悯

4. The Concept of Idolatry and Personal Idols
   偶像崇拜的概念和个人偶像

5. God's Efforts to Reveal Jonah's Idols
   神揭示约拿偶像的努力

6. The Process of Removing Idols
   去除偶像的过程

7. Conclusion: The Path to True Freedom
   结论：通往真正自由的道路
