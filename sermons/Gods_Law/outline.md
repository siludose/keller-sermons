# God's Law

## Sermon Outline / 讲道大纲

1. Introduction and context of the Ten Commandments
   十诫的介绍和背景

2. The origin of God's Law and its relation to God's nature
   上帝律法的起源及其与神性之间的关系

3. The substance of God's Law and the interdependence of commandments
   上帝律法的实质和诫命之间的相互依赖性

4. Combining vertical and horizontal commands
   结合垂直和水平命令

5. Inner and outer aspects of the commandments
   诫命的内在和外在方面

6. The importance of contentment and inner transformation
   知足和内在转变的重要性

7. The impact of God's Law on personal life and society
   上帝律法对个人生活和社会的影响

8. Conclusion and call for inner transformation
   结论和对内在转变的呼召
