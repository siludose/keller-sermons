# Orphans or Children

## Sermon Outline / 讲道大纲

1. Introduction: The Ten Commandments as a framework for a great life
   引言：十诫作为伟大生活的框架

2. The Fifth Commandment: Honor Your Father and Mother
   第五诫：孝敬父母

3. What does it mean to honor? Not affection, admiration, or obedience, but respect
   什么是孝敬？不是感情、钦佩或服从，而是尊重

4. Why honor is commanded: Parents as representatives of God's moral order
   为什么要孝敬：父母作为上帝道德秩序的代表

5. How to honor your parents: Take them seriously, forgive them, and be a child of the Heavenly Father
   如何孝敬父母：认真对待他们，原谅他们，并成为天父的孩子

6. Conclusion: The role of Jesus in becoming children of God
   结论：耶稣在成为上帝儿女中的角色
