# Secret Treason

## Sermon Outline / 讲道大纲

1. Introduction to the perception of the Ten Commandments as guilt-inducing rules.
   将十诫视为引发内疚感的规则介绍。

2. God's purpose in giving the Ten Commandments—to guide us to Jesus who fulfills the law.
   神颁布十诫的目的——引导我们走向那完成律法的耶稣。

3. Ingratitude towards God as a root of sin and an offense against Him.
   对神的不感激是罪的根源，是对神的冒犯。

4. Comparison of ingratitude to plagiarism and treason, showing its serious implications.
   将不感激与抄袭和叛国行为相比较，彰显其严重影响。

5. Cosmic treason and living as one's own authority, disregarding God's rightful place.
   宇宙叛逆和作为自己权威生活，忽视了神的正当位置。

6. The secret nature of this cosmic treason as described in Romans 1.
   这种宇宙叛逆的隐秘性质如《罗马书》1章所述。

7. Ingratitude leading to repression, darkness, and subsequent sinful behaviors.
   不感激导致压抑、黑暗及随后的罪恶行为。

8. The solution: Recognizing our debt to God and living in gratitude, leading to joy.
   解决方案：认识我们对神的亏欠并活出感激，带来喜悦。
