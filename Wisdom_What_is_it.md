## English Transcript / 英文原文

### Introduction: The Importance of Wisdom

Welcome to the Gospel Unliked Podcast. Many of the questions we face in life are complex and aren't always answered by simply following rules. Do I speak up now or do I wait? Should I take that job or stay where I am? That's why wisdom is so crucial. But how do we develop it? Today join us as Tim Keller explores how we apply God's wisdom to the everyday complexities of our lives.

> "Today's scripture reading is from the Book of Proverbs 1:9-22, 32-33."

**As you listen to today's teaching, we invite you to go online to gospelunlife.com and sign up for email updates.**

### What is Wisdom?

The Proverbs of Solomon, Son of David, King of Israel, for gaining wisdom and instruction, for understanding words of insight, for receiving instruction in prudent behavior, doing what is right and just and fair, for giving prudence to those who are simple, knowledge and discretion to the young, let the wise listen and add to their learning and let the discerning get guidance.

> "The fear of the Lord is the beginning of knowledge, but fools despise wisdom and instruction."

**Wisdom is competence with regard to the realities of life.**

### Why is Wisdom Important?

For the waywardness of the simple will kill them, and the complacency of fools will destroy them. But whoever listens to me will live in safety and be at ease without fear of harm.

> "People without wisdom, the simple and the fools, are not just simply unfortunate if you don't have wisdom. It'll kill you."

### The Problem of Wisdom

Jerome Kagan taught for many years at Harvard. He says, we now know that neurochemistry creates three basic, habitual reactions to threats. Some people are wired for what he calls, some are anxious, some are aggressive and some are philosophical.

> "Foolishness is bound up in the heart of a child."

**We're all naturally foolish. Wisdom is acquired.**

### How to Find Wisdom

The fear of the Lord is the beginning of knowledge, but fools despise wisdom and instruction.

> "Here's how you find wisdom. It's through paradoxical fear and the foolishness of grace."

**The fear of the Lord is a joyful fear. It's such awe and wonder and such love that you don't want to grieve him or do anything to avoid dishonoring him.**

## 中文翻译

### 引言：智慧的重要性

欢迎来到《福音未解》播客。我们生活中面临的许多问题都很复杂，并不总是能通过简单地遵循规则来解决。我现在应该发声还是等待？我应该接受那份工作还是留在原地？这就是为什么智慧如此重要。但我们如何培养智慧呢？今天，请加入我们，听听 Tim Keller 如何探讨我们如何将上帝的智慧应用于我们生活中的日常复杂性。

> “今天的经文来自《箴言》1:9-22, 32-33。”

**在您聆听今天的教导时，我们邀请您访问 gospelunlife.com 并注册电子邮件更新。**

### 智慧是什么？

大卫的儿子所罗门的箴言，以色列的王，为使人得智慧和训诲，明白通达的言语，使人处事领受智慧、仁义、公平、正直的训诲，使愚人灵明，使少年人有知识和谋略，使智慧人听见增长学问，使聪明人得着智谋。

> “敬畏耶和华是知识的开端，愚妄人藐视智慧和训诲。”

**智慧是对生活现实的能力。**

### 为什么智慧重要？

愚蒙人背道必杀己身，愚顽人安逸必害己命。但听从我的，必安然居住，得享安宁，不怕灾祸。

> “没有智慧的人，愚蒙和愚顽，不仅仅是不幸。如果你没有智慧，它会杀了你。”

### 智慧的问题

Jerome Kagan 在哈佛大学任教多年。他说，我们现在知道神经化学创造了三种基本的、习惯性的威胁反应。有些人被称为焦虑，有些是攻击性，有些是哲学性的。

> “愚蒙贴在孩童的心中。”

**我们天生愚蠢。智慧是后天获得的。**

### 如何找到智慧

敬畏耶和华是知识的开端，愚妄人藐视智慧和训诲。

> “这是你找到智慧的方法。通过矛盾的敬畏和恩典的愚拙。”

**敬畏耶和华是一种喜悦的敬畏。这种敬畏和惊奇以及如此的爱，使你不想让他伤心或做任何避免羞辱他的事情。**
